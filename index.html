Our Story

Built for Bharat's farmers

KrishiConnect was founded by Adarsh Jaiswal with a single mission: make it effortless for Indian farmers to find the right help at the right time.

From the fields of Uttar Pradesh to farms across 18 states, KrishiConnect is building a smarter, digitally-enabled agricultural ecosystem — one that promotes rural employment, reduces idle labour, and creates real opportunity for millions of rural Indians.

Whether you're a farmer looking for harvest help or a skilled worker searching for the next job — KrishiConnect is your platform.
